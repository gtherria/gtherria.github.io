Gates of Desolation Bakery is a special place where Metal Rock meets baking. Everyone loves baked gooods, even punks. Our housemade recipes are deliciously hardcore and fun for everyone to enjoy.

Target audience

The target audience is anyone who is looking to experience delectable baked goods with a twist. Music lovers and people looking for a unique bakery experience are welcome. All of our flavors are based off of your favorite metal rock names and are fun for the whole family to enjoy. Rock out to some epic baked goods.

Our goal is to create an inclusive community and a bake shop unique experience. We bake extraoridary sweets with flavors so intense your mind may melt. *Gates of Desolation Bakery will not be held responsible in any cases of mind-melting.*
